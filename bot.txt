Project Name: Anaya's Green Day – Smart Waste Management

Mascot:
Anaya is the central mascot of this project. She represents awareness and responsibility in solid waste management. The character is designed to help people emotionally connect with sustainability through a simple, relatable story.

Concept:
This project uses a storytelling approach where Anaya, an 8-year-old girl, demonstrates how small daily habits can reduce waste and improve environmental sustainability.

Core Message:
Anaya symbolizes smart waste management in everyday life. When users see Anaya, they should associate her with:
- Reducing plastic usage
- Reusing resources
- Recycling waste
- Sustainable living habits

Story Flow:
The narrative follows Anaya and her brother through daily activities:
- Using refillable bottles instead of plastic sachets
- Carrying reusable water bottles
- Converting food waste into compost or biogas
- Sorting waste before bedtime

Purpose:
To make solid waste management easy to understand, engaging, and memorable using a mascot-driven approach.

Goal:
To create a strong association between the character "Anaya" and sustainable waste management practices, so that the message stays with users even after viewing.
