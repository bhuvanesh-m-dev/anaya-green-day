Project Name: Anaya's Green Day – Smart Waste Management

Overview:
This project is a sustainability awareness initiative centered around a mascot named Anaya, an 8-year-old girl. The project uses a comic-style narrative to demonstrate how small daily habits can reduce waste and promote environmental responsibility.

Core Concept:
The story follows Anaya and her brother Arjun through a day, highlighting:
- Replacing shampoo sachets with refillable bottles
- Using reusable water bottles
- Converting food waste into compost or biogas
- Sorting waste before bedtime

Purpose:
To educate and inspire individuals to adopt simple, sustainable practices in daily life.
