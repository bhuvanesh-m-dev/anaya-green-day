# 🌱 Anaya's Green Day

> Small Habits, Big Impact – Smart Waste Management

A minimalist black & white storytelling webpage that presents a **comic-style sustainability journey** through the daily life of an 8-year-old girl named **Anaya**.

🔗 Live Project: https://bhuvanesh-m-dev.github.io/anaya-green-day

---

## 📖 About the Project

**Anaya's Green Day** is an awareness-driven web project that shows how **simple daily habits** can reduce waste and promote a sustainable lifestyle.

The project combines:
- 🌿 Storytelling  
- 🎨 Comic-style visuals  
- 💻 Clean web design  

to deliver an engaging environmental message.

---

## 🧠 Concept

The story follows **Anaya and her brother Arjun** through a full day:

- 🌅 Morning → Replace shampoo sachets with refillable bottles  
- 🏞 Afternoon → Use reusable water bottles  
- 🍃 Evening → Convert food waste into compost / biogas  
- 🌙 Night → Sort waste before bedtime  
- 🌍 Future → A cleaner and greener city  

---

## 🎯 Key Message

> **Reduce • Reuse • Recycle**  
> Small daily actions create a big environmental impact.
